Technical Specifications

What's Inside TinyLab

Autosampler

28 samples
  • Up to 28 samples at a time
  • Available filters
  • Available liquefaction module
  • Self-cleaning mode

Microreactors

Up to 24
  • Headspace pressure & gas control
  • Microfluidic mixing: Up to 0.1 μL accuracy
  • In-situ electrochemical measurement
    • DPV (Differential Pulse Voltammetry)
    • CV (Cyclic Voltammetry)
    • pH
    • TA (Titratable Acidity)
  • Independent temperature control zones: −10 to +125°C (+14 to +257°F)
  • Self-cleaning mode

Programmable Temperature Vaporizer

  • Heats up to +300°C
  • Modes:
    • Quick Ramp
    • Programmed Ramp Schedule
  • Split gas control: 1:1, 1:2, 1:3, 1:4, 1:9, 1:19, 1:99 (Outlet:Waste)

Gas Chromatography Columns

4 parallel
  • 4 parallel MEMS μGC columns
  • Unique stationary phases
  • On-chip heating/cooling

Sensing Modules

  • Flow-Modulated Pneumatic Resonator
    • Use a single spectrometer for multiple measurements
    • Control & divert gas flow
  • MEMS multi-pass optical flow cells
    • Gas: UV-VIS, NIR
    • Liquid: UV-VIS, NIR
  • MEMS single-pass optical flow cells
    • Liquid: UV-VIS, NIR

Multiple Photonics Options Available

  • Spectrometer options:
    • 200–1000 nm
    • 900–1700 nm
    • 900–2500 nm
