Why Tinylab?

01. Context

Businesses producing Agricultural, Biomanufactured, and Chemical goods power the American economy.

  • 1.9 million farms and facilities generate $8 Trillion in value to the U.S. economy.
  • ABC Businesses rely on 3rd-party analytical chemistry laboratories for regulatory compliance, quality control, process monitoring, and more.

Industrial Fermentation

Microbial fermentation processes are highly sensitive to small changes in temperature, chemical composition, agitation, and more.

The best way to monitor the health of the microbes and progress of the fermentation is through chemical sampling and analysis. This means getting chemical information quickly is essential for making live adjustments to the fermentation process to maximize yields.

High-end process probes and analytical instruments are prohibitively expensive and 48+ hour wait times for results from 3rd-party labs prevents immediate action.

02. Problem

3rd-Party Labs are Slow and Expensive.

  • Information is too slow to be actionable, increasing the risk of spoilage and loss and decreasing operational efficiency.
  • The per-sample pricing model is too expensive to gather enough useful data to increase yields.

04. Evolution

Anaco’s Chemical Digital Twin platform is only possible with Tinylab.

  • ABC businesses can create chemical digital twins that allow them to improve their production processes in a simulated environment the same way that modern manufacturers do with robots.
  • CDT-facilitated simulations enable commodity good-producing ABC businesses to reduce expenses, increase yields, and improve quality.
